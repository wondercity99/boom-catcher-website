— Privacy

Short, honest, and about one thing.

Last updated · April 27, 2026

This policy covers the Boom Catcher landing page and beta waitlist only. The app itself will have a separate, more detailed privacy policy at launch.

What we collect

When you submit the signup form on this page, we collect:

  • Your email address.
  • The date and time you submitted.
  • Which form on the page you used (hero or sticky footer) so we can improve the design.

We do not collect your name, IP address, or any other information beyond what's listed above. The form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service, and emails are stored there until we move the list to an email sender.

How we use it

One reason, one list: to email you occasional updates about the Boom Catcher beta — launch dates, invites, and a final "we're live" message. That's it.

  • We do not sell your email.
  • We do not share it with advertisers.
  • We do not use it for any other product or company.

Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link. You can also ask us to remove you at any time by replying to any email we've sent you — we'll delete your record within 10 business days.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you (for example, under GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or similar state laws). To exercise any of these rights, reply to any email you've received from us.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the beta list: reply to any email you receive from us, or check back here for an updated contact address closer to launch.
